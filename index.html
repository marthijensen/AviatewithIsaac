WELCOME TO AVIATE WITH ISAAC

More Than Flight Lessons. A Clear Path Forward.

Aviate with Isaac provides patient, student-centered flight and ground instruction for aspiring and advancing pilots. Isaac is an Advanced Ground Instructor (AGI), Instrument Ground Instructor (IGI), and Certified Flight Instructor (CFI) who holds a Commercial Pilot certificate for single- and multi-engine land aircraft.

His passion is helping aviation students succeed. Whether you are preparing for your first discovery flight, working toward a certificate or rating, studying for a knowledge test, or getting ready for a checkride, each lesson is designed to build practical skill, sound judgment, and confidence.

Isaac currently teaches as a Ground Instructor at Utah Valley University in a Part 141 environment. He brings a supportive, structured teaching style to every lesson and works to make challenging aviation concepts clear, approachable, and affordable so students feel confident, prepared, and motivated.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Starting Flight Training Should Feel Exciting, Not Overwhelming.

Here are a few questions new and returning students commonly ask.

Do I need flight experience before I start?

No. Many pilots begin with no flight experience at all. Your training can begin with a conversation about your goals and a first lesson or discovery flight.

Can I train around work or school?

Training plans should fit realistically into your schedule. Consistency matters, but your approach can be shaped around your availability and goals.

How much does flight training cost?

Cost depends on aircraft access, frequency of training, the type of certificate or rating, and individual progress. Contact Isaac to discuss your situation and available options.

What should I do before contacting Isaac?

You do not need to prepare anything complicated. Think about your aviation goal, your current experience level, and what you would like help accomplishing.
