COMMON QUESTIONS

Starting Flight Training Should Feel Exciting, Not Overwhelming.

Here are a few questions new and returning students commonly ask.

Do I need flight experience before I start?

No. Many pilots begin with no flight experience at all. Your training can begin with a conversation about your goals and a first lesson or discovery flight.

Can I train around work or school?

Training plans should fit realistically into your schedule. Consistency matters, but your approach can be shaped around your availability and goals.

How much does flight training cost?

Cost depends on aircraft access, frequency of training, the type of certificate or rating, and individual progress. Contact Isaac to discuss your situation and available options.

What should I do before contacting Isaac?

You do not need to prepare anything complicated. Think about your aviation goal, your current experience level, and what you would like help accomplishing.
